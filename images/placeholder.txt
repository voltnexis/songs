This folder contains images for the VN Songs website.

Required images:
- default-song.jpg (default song cover)
- default-artist.jpg (default artist photo)
- default-album.jpg (default album cover)
- hero-bg.jpg (hero section background)
- artist-bg.jpg (artist page background)
- album-bg.jpg (album page background)

Song covers: song1.jpg, song2.jpg, etc.
Artist photos: artist1.jpg, artist2.jpg, etc.
Album covers: album1.jpg, album2.jpg, etc.

Recommended image sizes:
- Song covers: 300x300px
- Artist photos: 300x300px
- Album covers: 300x300px
- Background images: 1920x1080px
